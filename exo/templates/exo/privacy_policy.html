ExoCollect Privacy Policy (Demo Version)

Effective Date: August 2025

ExoCollect is currently in a demo testing phase. This means that user accounts and data may be deleted at any time, and there are no guarantees of account recovery or long-term data storage. By creating an account, you acknowledge and agree to these conditions.

Information We Collect

When you sign up, we collect:

  • Username
  • Password (stored securely using Django’s default authentication system)
  • Display Name
  • Bio

No other personal information is required.

How We Use Your Information

We use this information only to:

  • Create and manage your ExoCollect account
  • Allow you to log in and interact with the demo platform

We do not share your information with third parties.

Data Storage and Retention

Your information is stored in our demo database. Because this is a test version:

  • Accounts and data may be deleted at any time without notice
  • There is no account recovery process

App Data & What Others Can See

Your in game progress and basic info may be visible to other players. This includes:

  • Display name & Bio(graphy)
  • Planets you have discovered

Security

We rely on Django’s default authentication and password hashing system to protect your account. However, since this is a demo, we cannot guarantee full security or long-term availability of your data.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support@exocollect.org.

What is Exocollect?

ExoCollect is a student/recent graduate led project dedicated to reigniting curiosity about space and space exploration. Our mission is to help people grasp the scale and composition of planets in the universe through an interactive gamified experience. As a virtual astronomer, you can discover uniquely generated virtual planets that no user has ever found before. Please note, this is an early beta version of our platform.
